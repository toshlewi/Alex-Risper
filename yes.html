Risper, my Valentine

I don’t even know how to write this without smiling like a fool. I just know that when it’s you, everything in me relaxes. Like my chest finally remembers how to breathe.

I love you in the small ways that don’t make noise. In the way I want to tell you everything first. In the way I catch myself thinking about you in the middle of random moments and my whole mood changes.

I love how you look when you’re happy. I love how you care. I love how you try, even when you don’t have to. I love your softness and your strength. And I love the way being with you makes me want to be better — not for show, not for people, but for us.

I’m not perfect. I’ll mess up sometimes. But I’m serious about you, Risper. I’m serious about loving you gently. About showing up. About listening. About choosing you, even on the boring days.

I want to learn you — your good days and your quiet days. I want to celebrate you loudly, and protect your peace softly. I want to be the person who makes you feel loved even when the world feels too loud.

So yes — this is me saying it clearly: I love you. I want to be your safe place. I want to be your person. And if I get to call you my Valentine, I promise I’ll keep loving you the way you deserve.

— Alex
